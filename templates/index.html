Essential Security Rules

01
Use unique passwords for every account. A breach on one site won't cascade to others.
02
Enable MFA everywhere it's offered. Even if your password leaks, attackers can't log in.
03
Keep software and OS updated. Most attacks exploit known, already-patched vulnerabilities.
04
Be skeptical of unsolicited emails and links. Verify sender identity before clicking anything.
05
Back up data using the 3-2-1 rule. 3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite location.
06
Use a reputable VPN on public Wi-Fi. Prevents eavesdropping on unencrypted networks.
